Overview Of Khan's Tomb

The image depicts Isa Khan's Tomb, a significant historical structure located within the Humayun's Tomb complex in New Delhi, India.
This 16th-century mausoleum serves as the resting place of Isa Khan Niazi, an influential Afghan noble who served in the court of Sher Shah Suri.

It is octagonal in shape and primarily constructed of red sandstone. The tomb is situated within the larger Humayun's Tomb complex, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. An adjacent mosque is also part of the complex, located west of the mausoleum.
