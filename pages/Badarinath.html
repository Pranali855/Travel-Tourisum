Badarinath Temple

The Badrinath Temple, located in Uttarakhand on the banks of the Alaknanda River, is dedicated to Lord Vishnu in his form of Badrinarayan. It is one of the four Char Dham and Chota Char Dham pilgrimage sites in India. Surrounded by the Himalayan mountains, the temple remains open only for six months due to extreme weather. The sacred hot spring, Tapt Kund, is near the temple, where devotees take a holy dip before darshan. Millions of pilgrims visit Badrinath every year seeking blessings and spiritual peace.
