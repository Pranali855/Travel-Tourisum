Akshardham Temple

Akshardham Temple in Delhi is a modern Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Swaminarayan, opened in 2005. Built with pink sandstone and white marble, it showcases Indian culture, spirituality, and architecture. The main monument has a large idol of Swaminarayan, along with carvings of deities, flora, and fauna. The complex includes the Yagnapurush Kund (largest stepwell), Sahaj Anand Water Show, and cultural exhibitions.
