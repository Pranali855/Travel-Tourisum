Overview Of Pavilion

Information about the Pavilion of the Enlightened:

Location: It is one of the 116 structures found in Ancient Siam, an outdoor museum also known as Muang Boran. It is specifically located in the Suvarnabhumi zone of the park and is numbered 110.

Design & Symbolism: This golden, lotus-shaped pavilion is dedicated to honoring 500 enlightened monks who achieved Nirvana.

Significance: It is considered one of the most iconic structures within Ancient Siam, recognized for its architectural beauty and spiritual significance, reflecting Thai artistry and Buddhist philosophy.

Recent Events: In 2006, the Pavilion of the Enlightened served as a runway for the finale of America's Next Top Model, Season 6, marking the largest model platform in the show's history.
