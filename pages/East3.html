Pelling, located in the picturesque state of Sikkim, India, is a charming town known for its scenic beauty, historical significance, and vibrant culture. Nestled at an altitude of 2,150 meters, Pelling offers a breathtaking view of the Kanchenjunga, the third-highest mountain in the world, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. Tourism in Pelling is booming, thanks to its proximity to mesmerizing landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and rich heritage sites. Visitors can explore the beauty of lush green forests, waterfalls, and trekking trails, making it one of the best tourist places in Sikkim.
